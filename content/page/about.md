IPAP Consulting was founded by experienced operating executives with a combined 30+ years across banking technology, cross-border payments, venture capital and large-scale corporate transformation. The thread connecting all of it is the same: putting together the right people, giving them the conditions to actually do the work, and getting out of their way.

## Where we've been

- **General Partner** at a venture fund
- **Managing Director and CTO** at a major cross-border payments business
- **CEO** at one of Eastern Europe's largest e-commerce platforms
- **Chairman of the Supervisory Board** at a non-banking financial institution
- **Independent director** roles at banks and clearing houses
- **CIO and Management Board member** at a leading regional bank

Several of those roles ran in parallel. Most of them came with a board, a P&L, a regulator, and the late-night phone calls that come attached.

## What we care about

**High-performance teams.** Not "high-performing" in a HR-deck sense — actually delivering under pressure, holding their nerve in transitions, and getting better at the work over time. Most of what we do is unlock teams that are already capable of this and have been getting in their own way.

**Change that holds up.** Anyone can deliver a programme that looks good in the closing meeting. The real test is what's still running 18 months later. We design for the 18-month test.

**Agile, but quietly.** We use the practices that actually move work — clear roadmaps, short feedback loops, transparent metrics. We don't sell certifications.

**Corporate accelerators that work.** Most internal innovation programmes are theatre. The ones that produce real product/business outcomes follow a pattern. We know the pattern.
