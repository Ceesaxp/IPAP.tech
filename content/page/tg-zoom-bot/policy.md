# Privacy Policy

*Effective Date: 2025-04-01*

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## 1. Information We Collect

  - User Data: Name, email address, and Zoom account details (via OAuth).
	- Meeting Data: Title, date, time, and participant emails.

## 2. How We Use Your Data

	-	To schedule and manage Zoom meetings on your behalf.
	-	To send notifications or reminders related to scheduled meetings.
	-	To improve our service.

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share your data:
	-	With Zoom via their official API, to schedule meetings.
	-	If required by law.

## 4. Data Storage

Your data is securely stored and encrypted. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service.

## 5. Your Rights

You can request deletion or correction of your data at any time by contacting us at [support@ipap.tech](mailto:support@ipap.tech).
